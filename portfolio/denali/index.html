Denali is powered by the Gantry 5 framework, a brilliant combination of power and simplicity that makes creating a unique website easy.

Gantry was born when the RocketTheme development team wanted to consolidate our extensive set of custom Grav, Joomla and WordPress theme functionality into a simple, easy to use framework. The result, a dynamic, easy-to-use framework that requires no coding experience to use. The only thing Gantry 5 doesn't do is compromise.
